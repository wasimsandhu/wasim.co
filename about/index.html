On January 2nd, 1999, I was born in Yuba City, California to two Pakistani immigrants and I've lived here ever since. I graduated from River Valley High School in 2017 with a 3.9 non-weighted GPA, four honors classes, and 5 AP classes.

Along with keeping up with my classes, I also started the school's first video bulletin, created geofilters for both the school and my town, and wrote education apps that me and my friends could use.

Right now, I'm a full-time student taking lower-division classes at community college so that I can transfer to UC Berkeley as a Biochemistry major. I also work at JCPenney part-time and as a tutor for chemistry and calculus at Yuba College.

My ultimate goal is to be able to go to medical school and become a neurologist so that I can discover breakthroughs about the human brain to help work on treating brain/nerve diseases.

In my free time I like to skateboard, play guitar, and draw in my sketchbook. I also like to binge-watch TV shows with 3+ seasons and read science fiction.
